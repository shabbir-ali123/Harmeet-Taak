Overview

TIBCO - Manager

Business Connect is an on-premise enterprise B2B platform that facilitates secure communication and transfer of messages and documents between different business partners and a central host. The application, initially built over 20 years ago, was in dire need of modernization to keep up with current technological standards and user expectations. I initiated this project alongside a team member and, after establishing a solid foundation, transitioned the project to my team in India for further development. My involvement spanned end-to-end across various phases, working closely with stakeholders, UX researchers, developers, and QA teams.

Project Goals

  • Technology Modernization: Transitioning from outdated technology to a modern stack, enhancing the application's performance, security, and scalability.

  • User-Centric Design: Creating an intuitive and user-friendly interface to streamline user tasks, reduce complexity, and minimize reliance on customer support.

  • Revenue Optimization: Improving the user experience to increase task completion rates, reduce operational costs, and drive higher business profits.

    Usability Testing and Success Criteria :

    I partnered with Usability Team for low fidelity testing with some of our existing clients/users to evaluate our designs. I have been part of observer and there’s couple of iterations happens between Testing.

    Analysis and Evaluation data gathered:

  • We have defined a series of tasks and asked user to perform the task in a prototype.
  •  I was observing the user while performing the task and asking them to loudly read out their thoughts while performing the task till the completion of it to get to know where they are facing difficulty and how their overall experience.
  • We did usability testing with 8 users and gathered all the info and their experience with the application by keenly observing them. This helped us to understand user better and what all areas needs to be improved.
  • With continuous iteration process of feedback correction and testing data we have finalized on the product with our last successful usability testing of final product.

    • Business Connect got launched on March 2020, and so far below are the findings for the product.

    Success Criteria :

  • Performance of the application has improved a lot with the latest technology.
  •  Calls with respect to the trouble facing by user while accessing the application with the customer service has been reduced drastically by 18% which helps even customer to focus on assistance needed problems.
  •  No third party tool is needed to decode the error messages and logs.
  • Too early to say regarding the growth of the product but target is at least 35-40% increase by 2021.
  • We have reduced the production time and training time of the application as the latest technology and user experience design of the product was easy for the user to understand all the task and perform their task to completion without any obstacle
