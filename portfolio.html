Overview

TIBCO-Manager

Business Connect is an on-premise enterprise B2B platform that facilitates secure communication and transfer of messages and documents between different business partners and a central host. The application, initially built over 20 years ago, was in dire need of modernization to keep up with current technological standards and user expectations. I initiated this project alongside a team member and, after establishing a solid foundation, transitioned the project to my team in India for further development. My involvement spanned end-to-end across various phases, working closely with stakeholders, UX researchers, developers, and QA teams.

Project Goals

  • Technology Modernization: Transitioning from outdated technology to a modern stack, enhancing the application's performance, security, and scalability.
