Overview

Sales Configuration Engine (SCE)

The Sales Configuration Engine (SCE) is a guided self-service tool designed to assist sales and non-technical professionals in providing customers with initial product configurations within 3 to 5 minutes. This tool simplifies the configuration process, making it quick and intuitive for users without deep technical knowledge.

Problem Statement

Sales teams and non-technical staff often struggled to provide customers with accurate initial configurations promptly. The existing process was time-consuming, complex, and required technical expertise, leading to inefficiencies and a poor customer experience.

Key Challenges:

  • Simplify the configuration process for non-technical users.

  • Ensure the tool is efficient, enabling configurations in under 5 minutes.

  • Maintain accuracy and flexibility in the configurations.

    User Research

    Research and Discovery

    To better understand the pain points and requirements, we conducted user research involving:

  • Interviews with Sales Teams: To identify their challenges and needs in the configuration process.

  • Competitor Analysis: To explore how similar tools are designed and where they fall short.

  • Stakeholder Workshops: To gather insights and align on project goals.

    • Key Findings:

  • Users found existing tools too technical and intimidating.

  • The configuration process was often delayed due to the complexity of the tools.

  • There was a strong need for a more guided and intuitive process that non-technical users could easily navigate.

    • User Persona

    Ideation and Design

    Based on the research findings, we outlined the key features and requirements for SCE:

  • Guided Navigation: Step-by-step guidance to help users through the configuration process.

  • User-Friendly Interface: A clean and intuitive UI that minimizes the learning curve.

  • Flexibility: Allow users to make adjustments as needed without starting from scratch.

  • Quick Configurations: Ensure the entire process can be completed in 3 to 5 minutes.

    • Visual Design

    Result

    Results

    The SCE tool was successfully launched and received positive feedback from users. Key outcomes include:

    • Increased Efficiency: The time required to complete configurations was reduced by 60%, enabling quicker customer interactions.

    • Improved User Satisfaction: Sales teams reported higher satisfaction with the tool, noting its ease of use and the reduction in errors.

    • Wider Adoption: The tool saw a high adoption rate among non-technical staff, who previously struggled with similar tools.
